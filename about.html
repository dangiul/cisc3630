About

Danny Giuliano (1997)
Fort Myers, FL.

Danny Giuliano is a Brooklyn-based artist pursuing a bachelor’s degree in computer science at Brooklyn College. Creatively, he is interested in portraying his perspective on the relationships between technology, art, class, and gender through audio and visual mediums such as music, photography, web design, and the construction of musical instruments.

Danny has received scholarships such as the Helen D. Henkin ’42 Memorial Scholarship and the Harold M & Carolyn R Scholarship (2023, 2024, 2025). He has also received grants from organizations such as Out in Tech and was chosen to participate in the PATHS mentorship program with LGBT Tech (2024). He is expected to graduate in Spring 2026.
